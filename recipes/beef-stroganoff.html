Beef Stroganoff

Description

This hearty beef stroganoff recipe is quick, easy, and sure to please! Using just a few simple ingredients requicing minimal prep time, you can make a delicious, creamy, comforting meal to feed the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) package of egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to tast

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. At the same time, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boild until tender yet firm to the bit, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasoinally.
  5. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
