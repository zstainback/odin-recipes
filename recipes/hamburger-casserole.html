Hamburger Casserole

Description

This hamburger casserole recipe produces a quick and easy one-pan dinner that somewhat resembles a simplified goulash. Featuring hamburger, elbow macaroni, tomates, and cheese, it is sure to become a comforting dinner-time classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef.
  • 1 small onion, diced (if desired)
  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • Salt and pepper to tast
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, sharp

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Prepare macaroni according to instructions on box.
  3. Brown ground beef and onion in a ten-inch skillet over medium-high heat, drain.
  4. Add tomatoes and soup to the hamburger in the skillet. Bring to boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer 5-10 minutes or until macaroni reach “al dente”.
  6. Drain pasta and stir into skillet.
  7. Empty skillet into casserole dish, top with cheese and bake 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven. Serve immediately.
